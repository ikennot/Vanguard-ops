Rules

1. Move freely across platforms and use jetpack pickup for short tactical flight.

2. Weapons use limited ammo. Refill through crates and ammo packs.

3. Taking damage causes knockback, but does not reduce lives directly.

4. Falling off the stage immediately costs 1 life.

5. Win by eliminating 15 enemies before your lives run out.

6. Defeat occurs when all 5 lives are lost.
